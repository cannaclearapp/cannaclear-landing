Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

CannaClear is designed to help users reduce or quit cannabis with more clarity, structure, and support. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

Information the app stores

Most personal progress data is stored locally on the user’s device.

  • Name or nickname entered in the app
  • Quit date, goal, and progress settings
  • Journal entries, check-ins, cravings, triggers, and notes
  • Reminder preferences

How data is used

  • Show your progress and daily statistics
  • Support journaling and check-ins
  • Enable reminders if you choose to turn them on
  • Support subscription access and restore purchases

Third-party services

CannaClear may use third-party services such as RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and entitlements. These services may process limited purchase-related or technical data needed to provide app functionality.

Data control

Users can reset their progress inside the app. For privacy-related questions or requests, contact cannaclearapp@gmail.com.
