Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

CannaClear is designed to help users reduce or quit cannabis with calm daily support. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

Information the app stores

CannaClear may store information such as:

  • Name or nickname entered in the app
  • Quit date, goal, and progress settings
  • Journal entries, check-ins, cravings, triggers, and notes
  • Reminder preferences

How data is used

This information is used to:

  • Show your progress and statistics
  • Support journaling and daily check-ins
  • Enable reminders if you choose to turn them on
  • Support subscription access and restore purchases

Third-party services

CannaClear may use third-party services such as RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and entitlements. These services may process limited technical or purchase-related data necessary to provide app functionality.

Data control

Users can reset their progress inside the app. If you have a privacy request or want data-related help, contact us at support@example.com.
